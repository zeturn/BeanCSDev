# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience level, or perspective.

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect differing viewpoints and technical approaches.
- Give and receive constructive feedback with care.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project.
- Avoid sharing private information without permission.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, intimidation, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Sexualized language, imagery, or attention.
- Trolling, sustained disruption, or bad-faith participation.
- Publishing private contact details, credentials, or sensitive information.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, pull requests, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct. Maintainers may also temporarily or permanently limit participation for behavior they determine to be harmful to the project or its participants.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the project maintainers through a private channel. Do not disclose sensitive details publicly unless the affected person has given explicit permission.

Reports should include:

- A description of what happened.
- Where and when it happened.
- Links or screenshots when available.
- Any immediate safety or privacy concerns.

Maintainers should handle reports promptly, respectfully, and with appropriate confidentiality.
